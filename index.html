About Us

Founded in 2024, PolyMaths was established by a group of five passionate students from Port Moody Secondary School with a shared vision: to foster a love for math among children from elementary to middle school. At PolyMaths, we aim to make learning math an engaging and enjoyable experience. Our mission is to help young learners grasp mathematical concepts in creative and fun ways, building confidence and curiosity in the subject. We plan to run winter and summer camps to bring math to life through interactive activities and personalized guidance.
